🧘 Breathing & Relaxation

Take a moment to breathe and find your calm

4-7-8 Relaxing Breath

A natural tranquilizer for the nervous system. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8.

How to Practice

  • Find a comfortable sitting position with your back straight
  • Place the tip of your tongue against the ridge behind your upper front teeth
  • Inhale quietly through your nose for 4 counts
  • Hold your breath for 7 counts
  • Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 counts
  • Repeat for 4 cycles for best results

Benefits of Breathing Exercises

😌

Reduce Anxiety

Activates the parasympathetic nervous system to calm your mind

😴

Better Sleep

Practice before bed to fall asleep faster and sleep deeper

🎯

Improve Focus

Increases oxygen flow to the brain for better concentration
